Q/A (Questions and Answers)

This is a collection of Frequently Asked Questions about Space Biology.

***

Q: What is the main challenge of long-duration spaceflight?

A: The main challenges are radiation exposure, microgravity-induced health effects (bone loss, vision changes), and psychological isolation.

Q: How can I help NASA research?

A: You can participate in citizen science projects, apply for internships, or submit your research proposal through our Publications tab.
