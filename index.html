Space Biologist

Focuses on how living organisms, from microbes to humans, adapt to or are affected by space environments.

**Skills:** Molecular Biology, Genetics, Data Analysis.

Mission Architect

Designs the habitats and systems to keep astronauts healthy during long-duration missions to Mars or the Moon.

**Skills:** Systems Engineering, Radiation Physics, Biomedical Engineering.
