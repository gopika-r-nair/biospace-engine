Life at NASA

Here are some aspects of working with NASA in space biology and human research:

  • Opportunity to work on cutting-edge experiments aboard the ISS
  • Collaborations across global research institutions and universities
  • Access to unique datasets and mission telemetry
  • Participation in high-impact publications and translational science

Future of BioSpace / Space Biology

The future directions and challenges in space biology include:

  • Multi-omics integration: combining genomics, proteomics, and metabolomics data
  • Machine learning and AI for pattern discovery in biological responses to space
  • Advancing human health in deep space missions (Moon, Mars)
  • Bioregenerative life support and closed-loop biosystems
  • Open science policies and community-driven resource sharing
