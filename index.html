Q: What is the main challenge of long-duration spaceflight?

               

A: The main challenges are radiation exposure, microgravity-induced health effects (bone loss, vision changes), and psychological isolation.

Life at NASA

               

Hear from NASA employees about their daily work and experience in the Space Life Sciences division.

               
                   
                       

Astronaut Health Specialist

                       

Works directly with flight crews to monitor and maintain physical and mental well-being before, during, and after missions.

                   
               
           
           
               

Future of biospace studies

               

This field is rapidly expanding to cover areas like personalized medicine in space, artificial gravity, and planetary protection.

               
                   
                       

Planetary Protection Officer

                       

Ensures we do not contaminate other worlds with Earth microbes, and that samples returned to Earth are safely managed.

                   
                   
                       

Space Biologist

                       

Focuses on how living organisms, from microbes to humans, adapt to or are affected by space environments.
